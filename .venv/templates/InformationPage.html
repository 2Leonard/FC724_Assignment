Reasons for collecting data on student progress at GIC.

There are several reasons on collecting data of student progress:

  • 1).Have a Better Understanding of Students' Progress:Through the collection of questionnaires, GIC can obtain more data not limited to grades and have a more comprehensive understanding of students' learning progress.
  • 2).Help Students to Make Progress on Academic Process:Based on the feedback data of students, school can identify areas students need to improve and take relevant actions to help students improve their grades.
  • 3).Improve the Experience of Study:By the collection of students' feedback questionnaires to improve the teaching quality, as to achieve the improvement of students' learning experience.

The benefits and purposes of the data collection:

GIC data collection process helps enhance academic programs and the overall student experience, providing valuable insights into student progress, providing targeted support and resources to improve educational outcomes.

  • Through the collection and analysis of students' learning progress data, we can effectively improve the teaching efficiency and effect. This information allows our staff to better tailor teaching programs to each student to address their difficulties.
  • Analyzing the learning data provided by students also allows us to further understand the learning patterns of different students, and helps us to understand which aspects of teaching need to be adjusted and optimized to continuously improve teaching efficiency.
  • Through the statistics of student learning, we can also track the progress of each student and provide timely help to those students who are experiencing academic setbacks. This can effectively create a better and more complete learning environment, so that each student can give full play to their talents.
  • The analysis of student data also enables us to continuously improve all aspects of our teaching, providing a better learning environment for students and enhancing the overall student experience.

In summary, the purpose of collecting student progress data is to promote the continuous improvement and improvement of GIC education system, provide students with a better overall study experience, and provide strong support for their academic development.
